Automatic Centimeters/Inches to Degrees Calculator

This tool can be used to easily convert your wheel/tire size information into useful data to be used by your First Lego League team.

STEP 1: Wheel Dimensions

Input your wheel diameter in millimeters *-OR-* You can pick one of the tires commonly used by FLL teams below.


EV3 basic tire - 56×28

Motorcycle tire - 81.6×15

Balloon tire - 56×26

Small tire - 43.2×22
